    Advanced Digital Design and Verification (ADDV)¶

    Instructor & Date

    Instructor: Sneh Saurabh
    Date: 12th August, 2025

    🎯 Course Objective¶

    • Develop a basic understanding of methods, tools, and technologies for designing and verifying complex digital systems.
    • Gain practical skills in advanced design and verification methodologies.

    📌 Expected Outcomes¶

    • Apply various tasks in pre-RTL design.
    • Evaluate trade-offs during design stages.
    • Implement advanced functional verification techniques.
    • Design and verify systems at multiple abstraction levels using CAD tools.

    🧑‍💻 Pre-requisites¶

    Tip

    • VLSI Design Flow (ECE313/ECE513)
    • C++ or OOP concepts
    • Familiarity with SystemC and SystemVerilog

    💡 Applications¶

    Where will this be useful?

    • Semiconductor industry (system level)
    • VLSI, CAD, FPGA, Embedded Systems, Computer Architecture
    • System-level design efforts & innovation areas

    🗂 Course Content Overview¶

    Week Topics
    1 Introduction; Digital systems; Software & hardware design flows; Manufacturing & test
    2 ESL Design – SystemC Models, TLM, Virtual Platforms, Platform-based Design
    3 Hardware-software co-design – models, exploration, partitioning, co-simulation
    4-5 High-level Synthesis – flow, CDFG, optimizations, scheduling, pipelining
    6 SoC Design Methodology – IP packaging, IP-XACT, RTL generation
    7-10 SystemVerilog, advanced simulation, formal verification, UVM, CDC/RDC
    11-13 Power management, UPF, design flows

    📚 References¶
